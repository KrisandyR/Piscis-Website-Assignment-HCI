About Us

Pisces is an interactive website dedicated to marine animal. It was created and designed for educational purpose. It all started in 2012 when we at Pisces wanted to give children the opportunity to have a better understanding of our living ocean animals. So, together with professional illustrators, we developed this project that you are now visiting. One of the most important objectives of our work is to promote conservation and protection of marine habitats and species through education. Curiosity killed the cat, but satisfaction brought it back.
